About Chase

The Show
Speaks for
Itself

Chase the Entertainer is a comedy magician based in Colorado Springs. He's been doing this long enough to know what works, and weird enough to keep it interesting.

The Story

For years he performed on the promenade at Fisherman's Wharf and the Pier 39 stage, building a show that could stop a circle of strangers and hold them for thirty minutes using nothing but a rope, a coin, and an orange with attitude.

He produced and performed in Street Magic Live, a weekly variety show in San Francisco hosted by the legendary Frisco Fred, and took his act to stages across the country from California all the way to Philadelphia.

He shows up with everything he needs to leave your event something people laugh about, wonder about, and talk about till you bring Chase back for the next one.
