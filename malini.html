Meet Malini

The Closer

He's an orange. He wears a tiny turban. He sits on a goblet stand under a single spotlight and he knows things he shouldn't.

Malini is the last thing that happens before the audience goes home confused and pleased. He does not explain himself afterward. Chase explains enough for the both of them.

He travels with a human named Chase. The human is tall and talks a lot. Malini does not talk. He does not need to.
