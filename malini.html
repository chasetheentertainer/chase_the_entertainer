Meet Malini

The Closer

He's an orange. He wears a tiny turban. He sits on a goblet stand under a single spotlight and he knows things he shouldn't. He has been to Los Angeles, Philadelphia, San Francisco, and several places he declines to name. He does not explain himself. He does not need to.

Malini is the Mind Reading Orange. He is the closer of the show — the last thing that happens before the audience goes home confused and pleased. He does not explain himself afterward. Chase explains enough for the both of them.

He travels with a human named Chase. The human is tall and talks a lot. Malini does not talk. He does not need to.

Malini has performed across the country. He keeps no notes. He forgets nothing.
