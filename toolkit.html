Three situations, three sheets. Pick the one that matches what you're planning and you'll have everything you need on a single page.

01
Outdoor Events

Fairs & Festivals

A show people stop for. Comedy magic, audience participation, and a mind-reading orange — run 4 to 5 times throughout your event day. Self-contained, low footprint, all Chase needs is a spot near foot traffic.

  • How many shows per day?
  • What does he need from us?
  • Is it family appropriate?
  • How does pricing work?
  • Can we bundle with close-up?

Hospitality Venues

Bars & Breweries

Magic that sells beer. Close-up sleight of hand table to table, or a seated 30-minute set for event nights. No stage, no announcement, no setup. Chase shows up and makes your slowest Tuesday more interesting.

  • How does strolling magic work in a bar?
  • Will it disrupt service?
  • What kind of space does he need?
  • Can we do a recurring weekly slot?
  • Can we bundle with a stage show?

Private & Corporate

Private Events

The one they'll remember. Strolling close-up for cocktail hours, a comedy stage show for the full crowd, or both combined. Scales from 10 guests to 200 and adapts to any venue without a tech rider nightmare.

  • What does the show look like at our event?
  • Can we combine close-up and stage?
  • What does he need from our venue?
  • Is it appropriate for our audience?
  • How do we get a quote?
