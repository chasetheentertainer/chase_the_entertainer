What Chase Does

Two Shows.
One Guy.
Zero Rabbits.

A full comedy magic stage show for crowds, and close-up sleight of hand for rooms. Both involve impossible things. Neither requires a stage if you don't have one.

01
The Stage Show

The Full
Thing

A 20 to 30 minute outdoor comedy magic show built for mixed crowds. Sharp sleight of hand, audience participation, dumb jokes, and one orange that consistently upstages everyone. Not a kids show — but the kids will watch anyway. Chase runs four to five scheduled shows across the day, gathers a crowd each time, and leaves a spot exactly as he found it.

Duration 20 to 30 minutes per show, 4 to 5 shows per day
Setup Own PA and full gear, completely self-contained
Space Any spot with foot traffic — not next to a music stage or in a dedicated kids area
Pricing Fee plus hat pass standard. Flat fee no hat pass option available. Rates on request.
Good for
Street Festivals Community Events Family Events Outdoor Fairs Fundraisers Fringe Stages

02
Close-Up / Strolling

Inches
Away

No stage. No distance. Magic that happens in your hands, with your borrowed ring, using a card you picked. Chase works the room table to table, or sits down for a focused 30 minute set for rotating groups. The kind of thing people lean in for and can't explain afterward.

Duration Strolling as needed, or 30 minute seated sets for rotating groups
Setup No equipment needed. Rubber bands, a coin, a deck of cards. Fits in a jacket pocket.
Space Any social environment — cocktail hours, dinner tables, bar tops, taprooms
Pricing Flat fee. Rates on request.
Good for
Corporate Events Holiday Parties Brewery Nights Wedding Receptions Speakeasies Private Dining Cocktail Hours
