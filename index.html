How This Works

What You're Actually Getting

A Proven Act

Not a hobbyist, not a party trick. Years of street stages, fringe festivals, and corporate events across the country. The kind of experience that makes people pull out their phones to show someone else what just happened.

Zero Headaches

Self-contained. Own sound, own gear, own setup and strike. You give Chase a spot and a time. He handles everything else. Your event coordinator will either love him or be mildly suspicious of how easy it was.

A Room That Talks About It

The goal isn't polite applause. The goal is the thing people mention on the drive home. Comedy and magic together do something neither can do alone: they make strangers feel like they were in on the same secret.
